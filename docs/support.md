## Mailing Lists

Teapot uses mailing lists to coordinate development and operations.

### Developer Mailing List

For development discussions, proposals, and patches.

- Address: <teapot-developers@desy.de>
- To subscribe, enter your name and email address on [the subscription page](https://lists.desy.de/sympa/subscribe/teapot-developers)
- Please read our [Code of Conduct](https://github.com/interTwin-eu/teapot/blob/main/CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md)
- Contribution guidelines: [Contributing](https://github.com/interTwin-eu/teapot/blob/main/CONTRIBUTING.md)

### Admin Mailing List

For deployment, configuration, and operational topics.

- Address: <teapot-admins@desy.de>
- To subscribe, enter your name and email address on [the subscription page](https://lists.desy.de/sympa/subscribe/teapot-admins)
- Please follow our [Code of Conduct](https://github.com/interTwin-eu/teapot/blob/main/CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md)
